Lasagna Recipe

Description

Tasty lasagna.

Super tasty and amazing.

Ingredients

Yield: 100 servings

  • 1/4 cup tomato
  • 10 lb mozzarella
  • 65 lb ground beef
  • 300 lb pasta sheet

Steps

  1. Mix everything together in a bowl.
  2. Blend the mixture with a food processor.
  3. Bake at 400c for 24 hours.
